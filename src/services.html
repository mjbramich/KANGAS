Pressure Cleaning

Pressure cleaning is a powerful cleaning method that uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt, grime, mould, and other stubborn buildup from various surfaces. It's like giving your home a deep and thorough clean! There are many benefits to pressure cleaning. First, it's incredibly effective at removing tough stains and dirt that regular cleaning methods might struggle with. Second, it can help restore the original look of your surfaces, making them look fresh and new again. Third, pressure cleaning can help prevent damage caused by mould, mildew, and other contaminants, which can extend the lifespan of your surfaces. Lastly, it's a time-saving solution, as pressure washing can cover large areas quickly and efficiently. The team at Kanga’s have the knowledge and experience to cater to all your Pressure cleaning needs.

Soft Washing

Soft washing is a gentle and effective cleaning method for your home's exterior surfaces. Unlike pressure cleaning, which uses high-pressure water, soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with specialised cleaning solutions to remove dirt, algae, mould, and other grime. The benefits of soft washing are quite impressive! These methods are safe for delicate surfaces like roofs, siding, and painted surfaces, as it doesn't cause any damage. It provides a longer-lasting clean by treating the root cause of the buildup, preventing regrowth. Soft washing helps improve the overall curb appeal of your home, making it look fresh, vibrant, and well-maintained. With our soft washing capabilities we’re able to provide our customers with amazing results and ensure the safety of your investments at all times.
