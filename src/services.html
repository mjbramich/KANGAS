Services

Pressure Cleaning

Pressure cleaning is a powerful cleaning method that uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt, grime, mould, and other stubborn buildup from various surfaces. It's like giving your home a deep and thorough clean! There are many benefits to Pressure cleaning. First, it's incredibly effective at removing tough stains and dirt that regular cleaning methods might struggle with. Second, it can help restore the original look of your surfaces, making them look fresh and new again. Third, Pressure cleaning can help prevent damage caused by mould, mildew, and other contaminants, which can extend the lifespan of your surfaces. Lastly, it's a time-saving solution, as pressure washing can cover large areas quickly and efficiently. The team at Kanga’s have the knowledge and experience to cater to all your Pressure cleaning needs.

Soft Washing

Soft Washing is a gentle and effective cleaning method for your home's exterior surfaces. Unlike Pressure cleaning, which uses high-pressure water, soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with specialised cleaning solutions to remove dirt, algae, mould, and other grime. The benefits of soft washing are quite impressive! These methods are safe for delicate surfaces like roofs, siding, and painted surfaces, as it doesn't cause any damage. It provides a longer-lasting clean by treating the root cause of the buildup, preventing regrowth. Softwashing helps improve the overall curb appeal of your home, making it look fresh, vibrant, and well-maintained. With our soft washing capabilities we’re able to provide our customers with amazing results and ensure the safety of your investments at all times.

Driveway Cleaning

Driveway cleaning will have your driveway looking fresh and clean. It gets rid of dirt, moss, oil stains, and other debris that can build up over time. Using high-pressure water, we blast away the grime, leaving your driveway sparkling and clean. Build up over time can cause serious injury due to slips and falls so, get ready to transform your driveway and make it shine like new!

Roof Cleaning

We use specialised equipment and cleaning solutions to effectively remove the buildup and ensure safety in the process. By keeping your roof clean, you can prevent potential damage, maintain its integrity, and improve the overall aesthetics of your home.

House Cleaning

We use our soft washing technology to apply our cleaning solution and rinse with low pressure to remove dirt, grime, mould, and other buildup from the exterior surfaces of your house. House washing can be done on various materials such as siding, brick, stucco, or wood, depending on the specific needs of your home.

Gutter Cleaning

Gutter cleaning helps prevent water damage by ensuring that rainwater can flow freely through the gutters and downspouts, instead of overflowing and potentially causing leaks or foundation issues. Regular gutter cleaning helps to prevent the buildup of debris, such as leaves, twigs, and dirt, which can clog the gutters and lead to blockages.

Soalr Panel Cleaning

Having your solar panels cleaned regularly helps maximise their efficiency and energy production. Dust, dirt, and debris can accumulate on the surface of the panels over time, blocking sunlight and reducing their ability to generate electricity. By keeping them clean, you ensure that they can absorb as much sunlight as possible and operate at their full potential.

Window Cleaning

Window cleaning is essential for maintaining a pristine and inviting home or business. Our professional service goes beyond aesthetics, offering tangible benefits for your property. Regular window cleaning enhances natural light penetration, creating a brighter and more cheerful interior.
